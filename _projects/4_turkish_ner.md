**Jun 2024 – Sept 2024** | Koç University (Repository Private)

*   Developed an annotation platform (**Yildirim**) for labeling named entities in Turkish tweets.
*   Annotated 5,000 tweets from a 700M+ tweet dataset to improve NER performance on low-resource, informal language.
*   Addressed challenges of slang, spelling variation, and complex syntax in Turkish social media.
